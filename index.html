What is Coredesi ?

Coredesi is local community app where you can find your nearest asian buyers and sellers. Find local restaurants that serve your authentic ethnic tastebuds, or find the zardousi you've always been looking for in a nearby store. With over 50 categories, this is just the beginning.

Follow Categories and get notified every time there is a new store that starts selling and save your most important stores and offices in your bookmarks. Buy and sell your household items or homemade food items in the request section.

Get The App

Download the app to find businesses and services near you. You can also add your business or home offering in the app for users to find out.
